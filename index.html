How to Play

  • It's your turn to play when your icon is highlighted, the first to play is chosen randomly.
  • You add a token in the targted column by double click in widescreens or single click in small screens. this can be changed in settings.
  • You win when connecting the choosen amount of grids vertically , horizontally or diagonally .
  • You can choose which type of connection to exclude in settings.
